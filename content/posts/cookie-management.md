## Motivation

We need a team management avoid **Cookie Hell** and **GDPR Compliance** problem.

\* Cookie Hell is Cookie request field over 4 KiB.

## Cookie Review Workflow

- submit to cookie management team

  1. justification
  2. life-cycle
  3. start-time to end-time

- submit to legal (if can)

  wait for approval
